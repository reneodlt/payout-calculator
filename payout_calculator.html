Poker Tournament Payout Calculator

Adjust the model parameter to control the payout distribution curve:
  • Convex: Positive values emphasize higher positions. A value of 1 is a straight line, less than 1 means all payouts are more equal, more than means bigger gaps
  • Concave: Negative values emphasize lower positions.
  • Exponential Decay: Reasonable values might be between 0.2 and 0.4. The higher, the bigger difference between each position
